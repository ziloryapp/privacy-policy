Privacy Policy - Image Merge

1. Data Collection

Our app does not collect sensitive personal information such as name, email, or address directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect limited information for functionality, analytics, advertising, and subscription management:

  • AdMob (Google) – for showing ads and measuring their performance. This may include device identifiers (such as IDFA) for personalized ads.
  • Firebase Analytics & Performance – to understand app usage, improve performance, and fix issues.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to collect crash logs and improve stability.
  • RevenueCat – to manage in-app purchases, subscriptions, and to process refund requests.

2. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Payments are processed securely through the Apple App Store. We do not access or store payment details. Purchase history may be processed by RevenueCat to unlock features, validate subscriptions, and manage refunds.

3. Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS devices, the app may request your permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to use the device identifier (IDFA) for personalized ads. You can allow or deny this request, and you can change your choice anytime in your device settings.

4. Refund Handling

When a refund request is made, RevenueCat may share your consumption data (e.g., whether you used the purchased feature) with Apple to help them decide on the refund. This is done in compliance with Apple’s guidelines and only for refund evaluation purposes.

5. Data Usage

The collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Improving app features and stability
  • Analyzing app performance and user engagement
  • Providing and personalizing advertisements
  • Managing purchases, subscriptions, and refunds

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the data collected about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian believes that their child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.
